# How to treat?

Treatment depends on the skin problem, but here are general tips:

- **Acne:** wash gently, avoid squeezing, use mild creams.  
- **Ringworm:** antifungal cream or tablets.  
- **Eczema:** keep skin moist, avoid harsh soaps.
